About Veritas

Trusted by corporations, legal professionals, and private clients for over 15 years. Our commitment to truth is unwavering.

Our Story

Founded on Integrity, Driven by Results

Veritas was established in 2011 by a team of former law enforcement professionals and intelligence analysts who saw the need for a private investigation agency that combined traditional investigative techniques with modern technology.

Today, we are one of the most trusted names in private investigation, with a team of over 50 skilled investigators, digital forensics experts, and intelligence analysts operating across the globe.

Our name — Veritas — is Latin for "truth." It embodies our singular mission: to uncover the truth, no matter how deeply it's hidden.
